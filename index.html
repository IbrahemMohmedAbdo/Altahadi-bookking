About Us

Al Tahadi Accounting & Bookkeeping is a company that performs auditing and financial advice for every company within the United Arab Emirates. We have multiple services under one roof, and we also have professional experience that helps you and covers all the services you need to make your business successful and advance your company. Our services vary in the areas of multiple and diverse accounts, financing services, feasibility studies services, and we have experience in administrative services necessary to improve business. Our goal is to provide many services to our customers according to their business needs by building a relationship based on trust at very reasonable prices.
